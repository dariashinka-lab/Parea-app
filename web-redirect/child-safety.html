Child Safety Standards

Parea App  ·  Last updated: 30 June 2026  ·  Version 1.0

Parea is an 18+ social platform for adults to meet in real life. We have a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), and we actively prevent minors from using the service.

1. Age policy

Use of Parea is restricted to people aged 18 and over. By creating an account, every user confirms they are at least 18 years old. We collect date of birth at sign-up and block account creation for anyone under 18. Accounts found to belong to minors are removed immediately.

2. Preventing CSAE

We do not allow any content or behaviour that sexualises, endangers, or otherwise exploits children. This includes — but is not limited to — child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, sexual extortion, the sharing of minors' personal data, and any content that depicts minors in a sexual or harmful context.

Any account engaged in such activity is permanently banned and reported to the relevant authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) where applicable.

3. Safeguards we have in place

  • Age gate at registration (date of birth required, under-18 sign-ups blocked).
  • Photo moderation on every uploaded image — automated review screens for unsafe content before the photo is visible to other users.
  • In-app reporting — every profile and chat exposes a "Report" action that routes the case to our moderation queue. Reports about child safety are escalated immediately.
  • Blocking — users can block any other user at any time, instantly removing them from all chats and event lists.
  • Account bans — confirmed violations result in a permanent ban that prevents the user from rejoining under a new account where technically possible.
  • No public minors — Parea has no "Kids" or under-18 mode and never markets to children.

4. How to report a child safety concern

If you encounter any content or behaviour on Parea that involves a minor or that you believe may constitute CSAE, please report it immediately. There are two ways:

  • In-app — tap the three-dot menu on any profile or chat and choose Report. Select the relevant reason and submit. Reports are reviewed by our moderation team.
  • By email — write to support@joinparea.app with as much detail as possible (username, screenshot, time of incident). We respond within 24 hours.

5. Compliance with the law

Parea complies with the child safety laws of every jurisdiction in which the app is available. Confirmed CSAE incidents are reported to the relevant national authorities. In the European Union we cooperate with hotlines under the INHOPE network. In the United States we report to NCMEC's CyberTipline.
